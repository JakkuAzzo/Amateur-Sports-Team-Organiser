## Suggested placement in this app
- Landing page hero right panel: one wide team photo plus subtle overlay.
- Login/register backgrounds: low-contrast training photo to keep forms readable.
- Dashboard cards: small thumbnail per event type.
- Open sessions page: optional looping muted MP4 banner (4-8s) at top.
